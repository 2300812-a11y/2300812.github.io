Educational Tour on Baguio City 2025, Baguio feels refreshing because of its cool climate, which is a nice escape from the hot weather in other parts of the Philippines. The city is surrounded by beautiful pine trees and scenic mountains that make it peaceful and relaxing. Baguio also has vibrant markets, like the famous Session Road and the Baguio Public Market, where you can enjoy local food and crafts. The city offers fun activities such as visiting parks, gardens, and historical sites, making it perfect for both adventure and relaxation. Overall, Baguio’s combination of nature, culture, and pleasant weather makes it a wonderful place to visit.
